The Aaroha Difference

What sets us apart is our commitment to authenticity. We go beyond the ordinary, sourcing our ingredients from indigenous communities practicing sustainable harvesting methods. This not only ensures the purity of our products but also supports the livelihoods of those who have been the stewards of these traditions for generations.

02

Our Philosophy

At Aaroha, we believe in the harmony of mind, body, and spirit. Our Ayurvedic products are crafted with care, reflecting the balance and wisdom of ancient traditions while embracing the needs of the modern world. We strive to provide you with more than just products; we offer a holistic approach to well-being

03

Ethical Sourcing

We take pride in our ethical sourcing practices. By working directly with forest communities, we contribute to the preservation of natural ecosystems and the empowerment of those who play a vital role in maintaining the delicate balance of our planet.

Services

Our Services

Abhyanga Massage

A traditional Ayurvedic full-body massage using warm herbal oils to promote relaxation, improve circulation, and balance the body's energy.

Panchakarma Detoxification

A comprehensive Ayurvedic cleansing program that includes therapies like Vamana (emesis) and Virechana (purgation) to eliminate toxins and restore balance.

Shirodhara Therapy

A soothing treatment involving a continuous stream of warm oil poured onto the forehead, stimulating the third eye, and calming the nervous system for mental clarity and relaxation.

Ayurvedic Consultation

Personalized consultations with experienced Ayurvedic practitioners to assess individual doshas (energetic constitutions) and provide tailored lifestyle and dietary recommendations for holistic well-being.

Herbal Steam Bath (Swedana)

A therapeutic steam bath infused with Ayurvedic herbs to induce sweating, open pores, and eliminate toxins, promoting skin health and overall rejuvenation.

Nasya Therapy

A nasal administration of herbal oils to cleanse and rejuvenate the nasal passages, enhancing respiratory health, and alleviating conditions related to the head and neck.
